## What is this resource?

A practical, fast-track guide to the core mathematics behind AI and machine learning. Instead of abstract theory, you learn the ideas that actually power modern models — vectors, embeddings, and self-attention — explained in an intuitive way with examples.

## Who is it for?

- Anyone using AI daily who wants to understand what happens under the hood.
- Developers and engineers who need solid foundations without a full analysis textbook.
- ML beginners who prefer intuition and practice over dry formulas first.

## What you will learn

- How data is represented as vectors and why that is the foundation of embeddings.
- How models turn text into numbers and what that means in practice.
- How attention works and why the transformer changed NLP.
- How these concepts connect to real tools (chat, RAG, classification).

## Format

A concise mini-book — short chapters, examples, and a focus on applications rather than proofs.
